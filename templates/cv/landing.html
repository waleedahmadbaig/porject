Services

How It Works

TumorInsight, inspired by my 5-step Business Intelligence model:

Clinical Contextualization

We begin by understanding your specific clinical setting, diagnostic workflow, and the types of brain tumor cases you frequently encounter. This ensures TumorInsight is customized to address your most pressing needs.

Secure Data Integration

TumorInsight seamlessly integrates with your existing systems (EHR, PACS, etc.) to collect relevant patient MRI scans and associated clinical data. Security and compliance with medical data privacy standards are our top priority.

AI-Powered Analysis

Our advanced algorithms meticulously analyze MRI scans to detect the presence of brain tumors, classify their type (Glioma, Meningioma, Pituitary, etc.), and generate detailed tumor segmentations.

Prognostic Insights

TumorInsight calculates data-driven survival predictions based on tumor characteristics, patient factors, and established clinical models. This information aids in treatment discussions and personalized care strategies.

Comprehensive Reporting & Management Tools

TumorInsight presents findings in clear, interactive reports designed for doctors. Additionally, the platform offers streamlined tools for appointment scheduling, tracking scan history, medication management, and monitoring patient progress over time.

F.A.Q

Frequently Asked Questions

  • What is TumorInsight?

    TumorInsight is a cutting-edge medical imaging platform designed specifically for the diagnosis and management of brain tumors. It provides doctors with advanced tools for tumor detection, classification, prognosis assessment, and patient care management.

  • Who can use TumorInsight?

    TumorInsight is designed for use by medical professionals who specialize in the diagnosis and treatment of brain tumors, such as radiologists, neurologists, neurosurgeons, and oncologists.

  • How does TumorInsight benefit doctors?

    TumorInsight streamlines the diagnostic process, provides accurate and detailed tumor analysis, offers insights into patient prognosis, and centralizes patient management tools. This leads to more informed treatment decisions and improved patient outcomes.

  • What types of MRI scans does TumorInsight support?

    Our TumorInsight System support MRI Images that can detect the Tumor inside the XR-Scan.
